Organic Color

Our salon offered customized organic color treatments tailored specifically to your hair type and desired look. Our staff is experienced in using only natural products that will nourish and protect your locks while providing you with stunning results.

Balayage

The technique involves sweeping or painting highlights onto the hair using foils and lighter shades of color to create an effortless, sun-kissed look. Balayage can be used on short, medium, and long lengths of hair as well as different textures such as wavy and curly.

Highlights

Highlighting is a hair coloring technique that involves lightening sections of the hair with bleach or other lightening products. It is used to create highlights and lowlights in the hair, which can add depth and dimension to your look.

Keratin

We infuse natural keratin protein into the strands, helping to strengthen and protect them from further damage. The process also leaves your locks feeling soft, smooth, and silky for up to three months.
